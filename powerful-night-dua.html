English Translation:

Jabir said he heard Allah's Messenger (SAW) say: There is an hour during the night in which no Muslim individual will ask Allah for good in this world and the next without His giving it to him; and that applies to every night.

Roman English:

Abu Sufyan se riwayat hai, unhone Hazrat Jabir (RA) se riwayat ki, unhone kaha: Main ne Nabi Kareem (SAW) se suna, aap farmate the: "Raat me ek ghari aisi hai, jo Musalman banda bhi agar is ghari me Allah se duniya aur aakhirat ki koi bhi bhalaai maange, Allah use zaroor ata farma dete hain." Aur ye ghari har raat me hoti hai.
