Statement of Facts

I received a pre-approved loan from HDFC and IDFC First Bank to purchase a car for Ola/Uber services. However, immediately after completing the taxi registration, the vehicle suffered a critical breakdown (engine pickup failure).

Despite having zero income from the car, I paid 3–4 months of EMIs from my own savings and spent over ₹50,000 on repairs. I even took a personal loan from Kotak to maintain my EMI history.

Current Status: The car is non-operational and parked outside my house. I have listed it on Facebook Marketplace. I will settle the loan immediately upon sale.

⚠️ LEGAL WARNING:

To IDFC & Kotak Recovery Agents: Do not harass or threaten. I have already sent a formal letter. If limits are crossed, I will hire legal counsel and file a case in the Telangana High Court using call recordings as evidence.

"Life is more important than money."
