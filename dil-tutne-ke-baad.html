Dil Tutne ke Baad Sukoon aur Seekh

Aisa nahi sochte ke "Mere saath ye kyu hua, mera kya qasoor tha." Aap ke saath jo bhi hua usme aap ke liye seekh thi.

Aap ne kuch seekhna tha. Agar aap ka dil toota hai to kya pata is dil ke tootne me khair ho. Kya pata is dil ke tootne ke baad hi aap ne bohot kuch seekhna ho. Kya pata wo dard sahne ke baad aap ko Allah milna ho. Allah ko bohot pyar hai aap se. Usne to har mushkil me aap ka saath diya hai, kabhi akela chhoda hi nahi. Us jaisa khayal to aap ka koi rakhta hi nahi. Jab sab gira dete hain tab wahi uthne ki himmat deta hai. Aur tab aap pehle se zyada mazboot ho kar uthte hain kyunki uthane wala jo hai na wo aap ka Allah hai, jo sab se taqatwar aur sab se zyada mehrban hai. Is liye mayoos nahi hote, kyunki Allah aap ke har gham ko khushi me badalne ki qudrat rakhta hai. Wahi hai jo mushkil ke baad aasani lata hai aur hamare dilon ko sukoon bakhshta hai. ‚ù§Ô∏è
