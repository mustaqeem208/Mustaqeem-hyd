Ye kahani Shaam mein pesh aayi, jise ek darzi ke malik ne bayan kiya hai. Kahani ka raavi kehta hai: main ek darzi ke karkhane ka malik tha, aur meri ek humsaye thi jiska shohar wafat ho chuka tha aur usne teen yateem bache chhod diye the. Ek din wo mere karkhane mein aayi aur kehne lagi: "Ae falan, mere paas ek silai machine hai jisper mera shohar kaam karta tha, aur hum is par kaam karna nahi jaante. Main chahti hoon ke in yateem bachon ki kafalat karoon. Kya main is machine ko aapke karkhane mein la kar aapko kiraye par de sakti hoon taake mujhe kuch aamdani ho aur main apne khandan ka pait pal sakoon?" Main uski baat sun kar sharminda ho gaya. Maine kaha: "Behan, bilkul! Aap isse mere paas bhej dein." Jab usne machine laayi, to maine dekha ke wo bahut purana model tha aur kisi kaam ka nahi tha. Lekin main us aurat ka dil nahi todna chahta tha. Maine usse poocha: "Behan, aap is machine ka kiraya kya lena chahti hain?" Usne kaha: "Teen hazar lira," aur ye waqiya jang se bees saal pehle ka hai. Maine usse teen hazar lira diye aur machine ko ek kone mein rakh diya. Das saal tak wahi halat rahi, har mahine Um Jameel aati aur machine ka kiraya leti, jabke machine karkhane ke ek kone mein pari rehti. Das saal baad, humne naya karkhana banaya jo qasbe ke kinare par tha. Jab saman shift ho raha tha to maine kaha: "Um Jameel ki machine bhi le jao." Manager ne kaha: "Sir, humein is machine ki kya zarurat hai?" Maine kaha: "Ye tumhara kaam nahi, le jao." Din aur saal guzarte gaye, phir jang shuru ho gayi. Us ilaqe mein sab kuch tabah ho gaya siwaye mere karkhane ke. Um Jameel se rabta kat gaya. Bohat koshish ki lekin uska koi pata na chala. Do mahine baad manager ne mujhe call kiya aur kaha: "Maine ek khwab dekha hai. Khwab mein suna ek aawaaz keh rahi thi: 'Falan ko kehna ke Um Jameel ki machine ki barkat se tumhara karkhana mehfooz raha'." Kahani ka raavi kehta hai: meri rooh kaanp gayi, ankhon mein aansu aa gaye. Maine kaha: "Alhamdulillah!" Waqai, mere karkhane se ek sui bhi ghayab nahi hui thi, halaanke poora ilaqa tabah ho gaya tha.
