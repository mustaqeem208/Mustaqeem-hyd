Charminar Ki History

Charminar sirf Hyderabad ki pehchan nahi -- ek purani dua ka nateeja hai. Jab Deccan me ek badi wabaa (ta'uni marz) phel gayi thi, to Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutub Shah ne Allah se dua maangi thi:

"Ya Allah! Agar ye wabaa khatam ho jaye to mai is jagah par ek masjid banwaunga."

Wabaa khatam hone ke baad unhone 1591 me Charminar banwaya -- pehle ye ek masjid aur madarsa tha, jahan taleem di jati thi. Baad me ye Hyderabad ki shan ban gaya.

Charminar ke chaar minar -- iman, ibadat, ilm aur amal ki nishani hain. Aur uske neeche ka bazaar, Mecca Masjid tak jaane wala raasta, purani tehzeeb ka zinda saboot hai.

Aaj bhi har roz hazaron log Charminar dekh kar bas ek baat kehte hain -- *"Hyderabad zinda hai."*

Note: Yeh maqala taleemi aur itihasi maloomat ke liye hai. Mustaqeem HYD News ka maqsad sirf Hyderabad ki asli tehzeeb aur roohani history ko zinda rakhna hai.
