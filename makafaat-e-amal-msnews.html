Ek shakhs tha jisne apni zindagi me kabhi bhi kisi ghair aurat ki taraf nazar utha kar nahi dekha tha. Ek waqt aaya ke woh shadeed beemaar aur tangdast ho gaya. Ghar me faqay shuru ho gaye. Us shakhs ki ek jawan beti thi. Jab faqay intahaa tak pahunch gaye, to wo ladki apne chhote behan bhaiyon ki haalat dekh kar ghalat qadam uthane par majboor ho gayi. Dil me iraada kar ke ghar se nikli ke apna jism bech kar unke liye khane ka samaan laau. Ghar se nikal kar poochti poochti us ilaqe me pahunch gayi jahan jism-froshi hoti thi. Wahan wo ladki ja kar apni ada dikhane lagi taake log us par tawajjo dein. Lekin kisi ne uski taraf dekha bhi nahi. Sham hone par wo mayus ho kar wapas ghar aa gayi. Jab ghar pahunchi to baap ne poocha -- "Beti, tum kahan gayi thi?" Wo raatte hue boli -- "Abu, majbooran gunaah karne gayi thi, lekin kisi ne meri taraf nazar bhi nahi uthayi!" Baap ne aansuon ke sath kaha: "Beti, tere baap ne aaj tak kabhi kisi ghair aurat ki taraf nazar nahi uthai -- to phir ye kaise mumkin tha ke koi tere taraf nazar uthata?"
Shayad isi liye Shariat sikhati hai ke apni nazar aurat ki chadar par bhi na daalo, kyunke nazar shahwat ka beej hoti hai. Aaj tum bacho ge to kal tumhari aulaad mehfooz rahegi. Jo boogay wohi kaatogay. Ye duniya makafaat-e-amal hai.
