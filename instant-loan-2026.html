Eligibility Criteria (Kaun Apply Kar Sakta Hai?)

In apps par loan lene ke liye aapko traditional banks jitni kathin shartein poori nahi karni padti:

  • Age: Minimum 18 years aur Maximum 58 years.
  • Citizenship: Valid Indian Citizen with ID proof.
  • Income: Monthly income proof (Bank statement ya Net banking access).
  • KYC: Aadhaar Card aur PAN Card hona anivarya hai.

Step-by-Step Application Process

1
Download App: Play Store ya App Store se official RBI-registered app download karein.
2
Profile Creation: Apna mobile number aur email verify karke account banayein.
3
KYC Upload: Aadhaar aur PAN card ki photo upload karein ya DigiLocker se connect karein.
4
Verification: Ek selfie lein aur apni income details (Bank Statement) submit karein.
5
Disbursement: Approval milte hi loan agreement sign karein, paisa seedha bank account mein.

⚠️ Safety Disclaimer

Sirf unhi apps se loan lein jo RBI Registered NBFCs ke saath jude hain. Kabhi bhi kisi app ko loan milne se pehle "Processing Fee" ya "Security Deposit" advance mein na dein. Fake Chinese loan apps se savdhan rahein.
