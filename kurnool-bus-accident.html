Kurnool Bus Accident: 20 Log Zinda Jal Gaye

Andhra Pradesh ke Kurnool me Friday subah ek bus me aag lagne se 20 log zinda jal kar halaak ho gaye. Yeh dil dehla dene wala haadsa poore mulk me afsos ka sabab bana hai. Tehqeeqat jari hai aur pehli report me yeh samne aaya ke Kaviri Travels ki bus ne kai transport qawaneen ki khilaf warzi ki thi, jiski wajah se yeh khaufnaak aag bhadak uthi.

Bataya ja raha hai ke Hyderabad se Bangalore ja rahi is bus me 234 smartphones maujood the. Forensic experts ke mutabiq mumkin hai ke in mobile phones ki battery phatne se bus me aag lagi ho. Is blast ne chand seconds me poori bus ko aag me lapet liya aur zyada tar musafir bach nahi sake.

NDTV ke mutabiq, in phones ki qeemat taqreeban 46 lakh rupay thi. Yeh phones Hyderabad ke ek tajir "Manganath" ne Bangalore ki ek e-commerce company ko bhejne ke liye parcel me rakhe the. Magar manzil tak pohchne se pehle hi yeh dahla dene wala dhamaka hua. Aankh dekhe gawahon ne bataya ke battery phatne ki awaazein aur chingariyan bhi dekhi gayin.
