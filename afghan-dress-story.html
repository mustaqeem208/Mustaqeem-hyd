Afghan Dress Story

Jis libaas par hamare mulk ke officers aur celebrities fakhr karte hain, Afghanistan me wahi libaas chowkidaars ko pehna kar unki asal jagah par khada kiya gaya hai. Angrez jab Mysore fatah kiya to ek tareeqi mehfil rakkhi gayi jisme waiter ko Tipu Sultan ki fauj ki uniform pehna kar Musalmano ka mazaak banaya gaya tha. Aaj wohi pehla badla poora ho gaya.

Yeh kahani hume yaad dilati hai ke history ke twists aur irony ko hamesha samjha jana chahiye.
