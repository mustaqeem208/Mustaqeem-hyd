Delhi Red Fort ke qareeb dhmaaka -- Hyderabad mein security on high alert

Kal shaam Delhi ke Red Fort Metro station ke qareeb ek gaari mein zabardast dhmaaka hua. Ibtidai reports ke mutabiq kai log hayat se haazir na rahe aur kai zakhmi huein.

Duniya bhar ki tarah Hyderabad mein bhi police ne security checks barha diye hain. Har entry point, metro aur public area par checking zoron par hai.

Hukoomat aur police se riyaasti updates ka intezar karein aur ghalat khabron par yakin na karein.
